Let's play Pig!

Pig is a dice game that can be played with 2 or more people. Each turn, you will roll the dice, and whatever you roll will be added to your total score. You may choose to roll the dice as many times as you wish... But watch out! If you roll a one, you will forfeit all of the points you earned during your turn, and the computer will take its turn. First one to get 50 points wins!

For more on this game see wikipedia's article. This is a variation on the 2-die rules.
